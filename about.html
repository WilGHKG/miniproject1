About Us

Marcid Blue is a medium-sized company engaged in the sale, franchising and management of water refilling stations nationwide. With the latest Reverse Osmosis Technology, Marcid Blue has the highest quality of clean and safe drinking water, as it is free from water-borne impurities that cause ailments and intestinal sickness. Our purified water is highly proven to be safe for all consumers, including lactating mothers and infants. We have the best-tasting purified water in the country too, as attested by our 100% of our customers.

Our Vision

To be one of the leading purified water companies in the country by utilizing emerging technology through innovation.

Our Mission

Nurturing lives by providing safe and clean purified drinking water to every household.

Who we are

We serve the Filipinos by providing safe and healthy purified water through our high-end machines that use the latest in water purifying technology and having a dedicated management team and committed water station franchises.
